Build a modern MOBILE APPLICATION for gym management and fitness tracking.

IMPORTANT:
- This is ONLY a MOBILE APP.
- DO NOT build a website.
- Use native mobile app UI/UX patterns.
- Optimize for Android and iOS.
- Use mobile-first layouts.
- Use touch-friendly UI.
- Use bottom tab navigation.
- Use stack navigation.

========================================
PROJECT OVERVIEW
========================================

Create a fitness tracking and gym management mobile app for local gyms in India that do not have their own apps like CultFit.

The app should support:
- Gym members
- Trainers
- Gym owners

The app should feel like:
- CultFit
- HealthifyMe
- Nike Training Club
- MyFitnessPal
